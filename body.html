Welcome to VCC 2018


The 1st Joint Workshop on Multimedia and Computer Vision Computing (MCVC2018) will be held at Anchorage, Alaska, USA, from July 23 to 26, 2018. The workshop is co-sponsored by

  • University of Texas at San Antonio, Texas, USA
  • Peking University, Beijing, China



    • Main Topic


    The purpose of the workshop is to bring together researchers and practitioners from Both China and USA, so that they can exchange their knowledge and stimulate each other through intensive discussions mainly (but not limited to) the research topics listed below.

  • Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning
  • Image Processing and Analysis
  • Machine Vision and Application
  • Speech Processing
  • Robotics and Application
  • Internet Technology and Visualization

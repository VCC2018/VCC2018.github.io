Welcome to VCC 2018

The 1st International Workshop on Visual Cognition and Computing (VCC 2018) will be held at Anchorage, Alaska, USA, from July 28th to 31st, 2018.

VCC 2018 will showcase high quality oral presentations and demonstrations from researchers from both USA and China. It serves as a forum to promote the exchange of the latest advances in multimedia, computer vision, and artificial intelligence technologies, systems, and applications from both the research and development perspectives of the circuits and systems, communications, computer, and signal processing communities.

The workshop is co-sponsored by

  • University of Trento, Trento, Italy
  • University of Texas at San Antonio, Texas, USA
  • University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China
  • Peking University, Beijing, China

    • Main Topic

    The purpose of the workshop is to bring together researchers and practitioners from Both China and USA, so that they can exchange their knowledge and stimulate each other through intensive discussions mainly (but not limited to) the research topics listed below.

  • Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning
  • Image Processing and Analysis
  • Machine Vision and Application
  • Speech Processing
  • Robotics and Application
  • Internet Technology and Visualization
